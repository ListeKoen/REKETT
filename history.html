HISTORY

REKETT is a band located in Denmark composed of five members - Jay (vocals), Danny (guitar), Bellz (guitar), Racer (bass) and Nick (Drums). The band was originally formed in 2011 by Danny and Nick and later introduced the other three members, with the latest being Jay joining the band in 2019.
