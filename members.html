MEMBERS

JAY

Jakob Sturluson, otherwise known as Jay Stoone, is the singer and frontman of REKETT. He joined the band in the summer of 2019 and is thereby the newest member in the band. Besides being the vocalist in the band, Jay is also composing lyrics for the band as well as being an integrated part of the song writing.

RACER

Rasmus Mejer, known as Racer, is the bassist of REKETT. He joined the group in 2014 as rhythm guitarist but soon after switched to the bass. He too is a big part in the composing of melodies for the band, as well as spicing up the music with his fenomenal bass playing.

BELLZ

Emil Nielsen, nicknamed Bellz, joined the REKETT in 2015. He is the rhythm guitarist of the band and works in close relation with Danny in composing melodies and riffs for the band.

NICK

Nicolai Hovgesen, also known as Nick, is the drummer of the band. As a founding member of the band, he joined in 2011 and has been part of the song writing ever since.
